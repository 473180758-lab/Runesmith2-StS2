# The Runesmith 2

A character mod for Slay the Spire 2, a rework of The Runesmith in Slay the Spire 1.

Mechanics:
- **Rune**: Runes are similar to orb. They trigger their passive abilities at the end/start of turns.
- **Recipe**: Recipe cards cost Elements to play. If you do not have enough Elements, you can still play the card to gain Elements equal to the cost instead.
- **Craft**: Create a Rune using Elements. You can have no more than 7 active Runes.
- **Elements**: Elements (Ignis, Terra, Aqua) are used to play Recipe cards or improve certain card effects.
- **Potency**: Potency is the power of a Rune when it is Crafted.
- **Charge**: Runes deplete 1 Charge to activate their passive abilities.
- **Break**: Destroy a Rune and activate its Break effect. Break abilities are typically double of passive ones.
- **Enhance**: Increase damage, Block and Potency of a card by 50%. Can be stacked additively. Enhance is removed after the card is played.
- **Stasis**: Prevent a card from losing Enhance when played.
- **Ice-Cold** (Debuff): Target loses 1 attack damage per stack. Stack goes down by half at the end of turn.
- **Amp** (Buff): Your next Crafted Rune gains additional Potency.
- **Brace** (Buff): Your next played card with Block gains additional Block.
