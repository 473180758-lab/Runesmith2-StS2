## Runesmith 2 2026-06-21 Changelog (v0.6.4)

### Content and Balances
- Assembler adjusted: Upgrade to gain Retain instead of reducing cost.
- Molten Chisel buffed: Deal 6(8) damage ⇒ Deal 7(9) damage.
- Steaming Chisel nerfed: Gain 3(4) Block ⇒ Gain 2(3) Block.
- Secret Recipe nerfed: Choose 1 of 2(3) random Recipe cards. Upgrade no longer reduces cost.
  - Note: Mundus is removed from the blacklist. It can now be offered by Secret Recipe as a 'low roll'.
- Chisel Stab renamed to Chisel Strike.
- Gamma Ray Burst buffed: Now trigger the passive effect of ALL Runes X(+1) times.
- Watch Out! adjusted: Give another player 5(7) Brace. Enhance 2(3) cards in the player's hand at random.
