## Runesmith 2 2026-05-28 Changelog (v0.3.0)

### Balances
- Hit and Run buffed: Deal 6(8) damage ⇒ Deal 8(11) damage.
- Curtain of Flames buffed: Gain 2(3) Ignis ⇒ Gain 3(4) Ignis.
- Chroma Chisel adjusted: Deal 7(9) Damage. Gain 1 of each Elements ⇒ Deal 7(8) damage. Gain 1(2) of each Elements.
- Stabilize buffed: Gain 6(7) Block ⇒ Gain 7(9) Block.
- Electrocute adjusted: Deal 9(13) damage. Stasis a card in your Draw Pile ⇒ Deal 8(12) damage. Stasis a card in your Draw Pile put it on top.
- Static Cage buffed: Gain 7(9) Block ⇒ Gain 8(10) Block.
- Makeshift Armor nerfed: Gain 8(11) Block ⇒ Gain 7(10) Block.
- Dry Ice buffed: Gain 4(6) Block. Apply 2(3) Ice-Cold. ⇒ Gain 5(6) Block. Apply 2(4) Ice-Cold.
- White Balance adjusted: Gain 1 of each Elements. Deal damage equals to 2(3) times the number of Elements gained this turn.
- Prism reworked: Attack - Cost 1 Energy and 1|1|1 Elements - Deal 8(11) damage. Gain 6(8) Block. Draw 1(2) cards.
- Gladius (formerly Lucky 7) buffed: Deal 35(49) damage ⇒ deal 40(50) damage.
- Superposition nerfed: Deal 15(21) damage ⇒ 15(20) damage. | Gain 11(16) Block ⇒ 11(15) Block. 
- Hammer Tornado nerfed: Deal 9(13) damage ⇒ 9(12) damage.
- All in One adjusted: Upgrade reduces cost to 0 instead of removing Exhaust.
- Secret Recipe will no longer offer Mundus in the choice.
